Mac Avionics, Inc. has been providing quality service to general aviation at reasonable prices since it was established in 1985. Through the years, aviation has changed, and Mac Avionics has changed with it.

Mac Avionics is well versed in not only legacy equipment but also the latest digital offerings from Garmin, Avidyne, Aspen, L-3, Bendix/King, PS Engineering, and Genesys. With our broad base of knowledge we can help you select the best plan to put your aircraft back in working order or bring it up to today's latest capabilities.

With capabilities that include custom panel fabrication and powder coating, we specialize in refurbishing aircraft.

Mac Avionics is located in the Double M Aviation complex at Lakeland Linder Regional Airport. Double M supports Mac Avionics when airframe needs arise while completing your modernization project. Double M also is happy to complete your maintenance needs while Mac Avionics takes care of your avionics needs. Double M hopes to also maintain your aircraft on an ongoing basis.

Bring your aircraft to a team that can handle the whole project, not just the easy parts.
