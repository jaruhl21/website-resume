Electronic Flight Display Installation

With several displays available from different manufacturers, Mac Avionics can find a product that fits your needs and budget. Giving you more data for better decision making, an electronic flight display can bring airline category capabilities to your general aviation cockpit.

Backup Electronic Display Installation

As a companion to an electronic flight display or as a backup to steam gauges, backup displays can eliminate steam gauges in some instances or give your cockpit a modern twist while still leaving the steam gauges you're used to.

ADS-B Equipment Installation

ADS-B out equipage will be required to fly in some airspace (including Class A, B and C)beginning January 1, 2020. Call us for information and to discuss what options you have for upgrade.

Audio Panel Installation

While typically the most overlooked upgrade, audio panels are the foundation of your avionics installation. With a modern and properly installed audio panel, your flights will be more relaxing and comfortable with modern audio panels that provide superior audio quality and fidelity. Many audio panels now offer Bluetooth connectivity for a cord free environment.

Autopilot System Installation

Although one of the biggest investments you might make to your aging aircraft, autopilot systems not only gives the pilot some relief on long cross country flights; they also add a large safety margin during IMC as well as when VFR workloads surge.
